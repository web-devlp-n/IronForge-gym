Our Story

WE BUILD CHAMPIONS

IronForge was founded in 2017 with one vision: to create a space where ordinary people achieve extraordinary results. From a small 2,000 sq ft facility, we've grown into a 15,000 sq ft powerhouse of fitness with over 1,200 active members.

Our philosophy is simple — great equipment, greater coaching, and the greatest community. We believe fitness is a journey, not a destination, and we're here every step of the way.

Whether you're a first-timer walking through our doors or a competitive athlete pushing limits, IronForge is your home.
